Rohan Agarwal

I am a Masters in Computer Vision student at Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, where I am advised by Prof. Jun-Yan Zhu.

My current research focuses on democratizing access to deep generative models and making it easier for researchers and artists to find them. Previously, I was an Applied Science intern at Amazon Studios where I worked on Dynamic Neural Radiance Fields for Neural Avatars.

Prior to joining CMU, I was at Qualcomm for 3 years in their Multimedia and Graphics team. My work there was focused on developing on-device, real-time computer vision solutions for mobile display. I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology, BHU, India with a major in Electronics Engineering in 2018.
